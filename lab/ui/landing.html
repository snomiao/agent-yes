Drive any AI coding CLI on autopilot.

agent-yes wraps Claude, Codex, Gemini, Copilot and more — auto-answering their prompts so they keep working unattended. Then ay serve streams a live, end-to-end-encrypted console to your browser so you can watch, steer, and spawn agents from anywhere.

Wrappers: claude-yes · codex-yes · gemini-yes · copilot-yes · cursor-yes · qwen-yes · grok-yes — or just ay.

Auto-yes the prompts

Trust dialogs, theme pickers, "continue?", overload retries — handled, so the agent never stalls waiting on you.

Live console, anywhere

ay serve shares a WebRTC console at /w/ — list, tail, and steer every agent from your phone or laptop.

End-to-end encrypted

The share link's secret never reaches the signaling server; traffic is AES-GCM between your browser and your machine.

Many CLIs, one wrapper

One tool across Claude, Codex, Gemini, Copilot, Cursor, Qwen, Grok — same flags, same live console.
